Paediatrics

This is a branch of medicine that focuses on the prevention diagnosis, treatment, and management of all types of health problems affecting infants, children and adolescents from birth up to the age of 18years

Orthopaedics

This is a branch of medicine that specializes on diagnosis correction, prevention and treatment of patients with skeletal deformities – all aspects of health care per taking to the musculoskeletal system

General Surgery

It is a specialty that focuses on alimentary canal and abdominal contents including esophagus, stomach, small intestine. They also deal with diseases involving the skin, breast, soft tissue, trauma and Hermia’s and perform endoscopic such as gastroscopy, colonoscopy and laparoscopic procedures

About Us

SLMC Medical Center was set up in 2003 with a vision of providing quality Health Care Services to corporate organizations and individuals within a cross section, at affordable costs. The organization was set up in Kisumu to provide distinctive services to the locals and beyond the Kisumu boundaries.

Vision

To create a society where more people are able to access quality and affordable medical services through offering high quality medical services to all without any discrimination at affordable rates.

Mission statement

Strive to provide quality health care to all through proper diagnosis of their ailments, put them on manageable treatment paths, support them and ensure they achieve their full recovery at a fair cost.

Janitorial Department

Responsible for cleaning and sanitizing patient rooms & departments using proper methods, equipment’s & chemicals. Empty trash, waste baskets & linen hampers & transport soiled linens

Laundry Department

Responsible for all laundry services at the hospital & all laundry services are done as per the standard policies & is met on time.

Transport/Ambulance Services

The Ambulance driver & paramedic personnel help in safe transport of victims & patients to the destined location. They provide first aid assistance to the injured where-in their goal is to stabilize the person before transferring them to the hospital for further medical care.

Nursing Department

Nurses serve their communities by providing evidence based treatment & wellness education by promoting healthy lifestyle advocates for patients.

Pharmacy Department

This department manages the use of medications in hospitals & other medical centers, they are involved with selection, prescriptions, procuration, delivery, administration & review of medication to optimize patient outcomes.

Laboratory Department

Laboratory Investigations are a vital part of the clinical assessment & laboratory results that guide the selection of drugs for patient management.

Administration Department

Directs, supervise & evaluate work activities of medical, nursing, technical, clerical, service, maintenance and other staff.

Accounts Department

Accounts specialist who are knowledgeable on billing and insurance processes.

Marketing Department

Marketing executives have the responsibility of planning directly, coordinating an organizations marketing strategies to ensure advertising initiatives are meeting targeted goals.

Catering Department

They provide a service of food & beverage to staff, patients and other visitors in the hospital
